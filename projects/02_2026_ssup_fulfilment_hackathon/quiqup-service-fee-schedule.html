Payment terms
Payment is due within 1 day of invoice issue date.
Payment method
Automatic Card Payment
The registered card on your account will be automatically charged. If payment fails, we will retry daily for 30 days. After the second failed attempt, service will be suspended until payment is received.
COD reconciliation
COD remittance will take place on a monthly basis. Along with your invoice, you will receive a Delivery report and a COD report.

Once we receive your written confirmation on the cash collected, we will:
  • Perform a net-off between your invoice and the COD amount collected
  • Settle any balance if the COD collected exceeds the invoice value

Other Terms & Conditions

Quarterly Price Review

A ramp-up period of three (3) months from the Effective Date ("Ramp-Up Period") shall be provided to the Customer to reach the Expected Volume. During the Ramp-Up Period, pricing shall remain fixed as per the initial rate.

At the end of each calendar quarter, the actual volume of services used by the Customer will be reviewed:

  • If actual volumes are equal to or greater than the Expected Volume, the current pricing will remain in effect.
  • If actual volumes are below the Expected Volume (after the Ramp-Up Period), Quiqup reserves the right to adjust the pricing upward to reflect the lower volume.

Liability

Please refer to the Terms & Conditions - Paragraph 15 for all provisions related to liability undertaken by Quiqup under this service.

The liability value is limited to the minimum of the liability cap of AED 100 per item or the CIF (cost of goods at source, freight, customs) value of the goods.
