## Open Questions

### 1. Why did Visit → Signup conversion drop in Q4 2025?

**Identified:** 2026-01-30
**Priority:** High
**Context:** Website → Signup Page → Account funnel analysis

**Observation:**
- Q1 2025 Visit→Signup: ~23.5%
- Q4 2025 Visit→Signup: ~10.2%
- **57% decline** in conversion rate

**Potential hypotheses to test:**
1. Traffic quality change (more top-of-funnel/awareness traffic?)
2. Website/UX changes to signup flow
3. Seasonality effects
4. Campaign mix shift (Performance Max vs Search)
5. Tracking changes or GA4 implementation issues

**Data needed:**
- Traffic source breakdown by month (paid vs organic)
- Campaign-level conversion rates to signup
- Website changelog for signup pages
- Compare bounce rates on signup page over time

**Related finding:** Signup→Account conversion *improved* in Q4 (51% vs 42% average), suggesting higher quality visitors who do reach signup are more likely to complete.
